Patents and Inventions

My practice is limited to the specialty of neurosurgery, which is the examination, diagnosis and treatment of brain and spinal disorders. He received his medical education at Thomas Jefferson University, where he also completed his residency training. Dr. Gorsen has been in private practice since 1988. He is a member of The Neurosurgical Society of the Virginias, Washington Academy of Neurosurgery, Medical Society of Northern Virginia and Congress of Neurological Surgeons.
